# Product Overview

"养只小马" (Raise a Pony) is a Chinese New Year-themed virtual pet application celebrating the Year of the Horse (2026). Users adopt and care for a digital pony through interactive gameplay.

## Core Features

- Fortune-telling onboarding flow that determines user's pony characteristics
- Virtual pet care system with hunger and happiness mechanics
- Growth and evolution system based on feeding and interaction
- Decoration collection and customization
- Five mini-games with Chinese New Year themes
- Daily check-in and achievement systems
- Social features including friend assist/boost mechanics
- Share card generation for social media

## User Experience

The app uses a warm, friendly tone with Chinese cultural elements. All UI text is in Simplified Chinese. The design emphasizes casual gameplay with gentle progression mechanics and positive reinforcement through achievements and visual rewards.

## Monetization Model

Demo version with no monetization. Fragment-based crafting system could support future IAP integration.
